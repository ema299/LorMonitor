# Mail DKP - Showcasing V3

## Obiettivo
Presentare la V3 come tool di analytics per la community competitiva di Lorcana, chiedendo feedback su utilita', posizionamento e compliance, non una concessione formale sui dati.

## Subject
`Early preview: V3 analytics tool for Lorcana community`

## Bozza

```text
Hi DKP team,

I am reaching out to share an early preview of a Lorcana analytics tool we have been building for the competitive community.

The goal is simple: help players, testers, creators, and organizers understand matchup trends, curve quality, mulligan decisions, and key lines of play in a more structured way.

The product is independent and community-focused. It is not presented as an official tool, and we are careful about wording, attribution, and compliance.

At this stage I would value your feedback on three things:
- whether the tool is genuinely useful for the community
- whether the positioning feels clear and appropriate
- whether there are any compliance or brand concerns we should address before we go further

If useful, I would be happy to share a private demo and walk you through the experience.

Best,
[your name]
```

## Versione piu' business

```text
Hi DKP team,

We have been building a Lorcana analytics product aimed at the competitive and creator ecosystem.
It focuses on matchup insight, curve analysis, mulligan support, and decision support for players and testers.

We would like to share a private demo and get your view on whether the product is useful, how it should be positioned, and whether there are any brand or compliance issues we should keep in mind.

The intention is to build something that is useful to the community and respectful of the ecosystem around Lorcana.

If this sounds relevant, I would be glad to send a short demo and discuss it further.

Best,
[your name]
```

## Note operative

- Non aprire con "usiamo dati Duels".
- Evitare qualsiasi formula che suggerisca partnership, endorsement o approvazione ufficiale.
- Se serve, usare la formula `community / public match data` invece di `Duels data`.
- La call-to-action deve essere una demo e feedback, non una richiesta di permesso commerciale.
