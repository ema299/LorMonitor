About Lorcana Monitor

Lorcana Monitor is a free analytics and coaching platform for Disney Lorcana TCG players. It aggregates match statistics, derives playbooks and coaching insights, and provides tools to review your own games.

Unofficial fan-made tool. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Disney or Ravensburger. Disney Lorcana TCG is a trademark of Disney and Ravensburger.

Data Sources

  • Community match data — publicly available match information and aggregated meta statistics from non-official Lorcana community platforms.
  • Public tournament results — competitive tournament results and reference decklists shared publicly by the community.
  • Your own uploads — .replay.gz files you explicitly upload to Board Lab for personal and coaching review.

Privacy

We distinguish between two data flows:

  • Public Replay Viewer — match logs from public community platforms are shown with nicknames anonymized: "Player" and "Opponent" / "Opponent A, B...". Raw nicknames are never exposed in the public API responses. External profile links are not generated.
  • Board Lab uploads — replays you upload are private by default. They are accessible only to you, and to any coach you explicitly share with. You can delete any replay at any time from your account. Uploads require a one-time consent.

User data we store

  • Account data: email, hashed password, tier, display name.
  • Preferences: language, theme, pinned decks, optional community-platform nicknames you provide for personal-stats lookup, optional country.
  • Consents: timestamped acceptance of Terms, Privacy, replay upload.
  • Saved decks you create.
  • Board Lab replay uploads (owned by you; deletable).

We do not collect credit card or payment information at this time (no billing provider is live). If and when we enable paid tiers, payment data will be handled exclusively by a PCI-compliant third-party payment processor; we never store card numbers ourselves.

GDPR rights

You can export all your data as JSON (GET /api/user/export) and you can request deletion of your account. Deletion marks the account and purges personal data after a 30-day grace window during which you can reverse the request. Your uploaded replays are hard-deleted at that point.

What we do NOT do

  • We don't sell cards, rank players for money, or run paid tournaments.
  • We don't publish your own match replays unless you explicitly choose to share.
  • We don't expose your Lorcana nickname outside your own account context.
  • Card images are displayed for commentary, analysis, and educational purposes under fair-use principles, loaded directly from public community CDNs. We do not redistribute or re-host them. We don't use official Disney or Lorcana logos.
