Data Sources
Aggregated from public community match data and matches collected across SET11, PRO and TOP categories. Reference decklists from public tournament results.

Scope
SET11 High ELO — MMR ≥ 1300, ~4-5K matches/day.
TOP — Top 100 players, MMR ≥ 1600, ~500 matches.
PRO — Top 50 players, ~70 matches.
Community — weekly community match data, ~60K+ matches.

Abbreviations
WR = Win Rate · OTP = On The Play · OTD = On The Draw · MMR = Matchmaking Rating
Ink: Ab=Amber, Amy=Amethyst, Em=Emerald, R=Ruby, Sa=Sapphire, St=Steel.

Privacy
Player nicknames from public match logs are anonymized in the public Replay Viewer ("Player" / "Opponent"). Your own uploaded replays are stored privately and accessible only to you and coaches you explicitly share with. See About for the full policy and data sources.
