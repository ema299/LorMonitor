Data Sources

Aggregated data from duels.ink (weekly community stats) and matches collected automatically across SET11, PRO and TOP categories.

Reference "standard" decklists from inkdecks.com (official tournaments).

Scope Definitions

SET11 High ELO — All Set 11 matches with average MMR ≥ 1300. ~4000-5000 matches/day, representative of the competitive ladder meta.

TOP — Top player matches at high MMR (≥1600). ~500 match sample, indicative of the optimal ladder meta.

PRO — Recognized pro players (27 monitored). Small sample (~70 matches) but representative of top competitive choices.

Community — Weekly aggregated stats from duels.ink, ~60,000+ matches. Represents the entire online meta, including casual players.

Abbreviations

WR = Win Rate | OTP = On The Play (first player) | OTD = On The Draw (second player) | MMR = Matchmaking Rating

Ink: Ab=Amber, Amy=Amethyst, Em=Emerald, R=Ruby, Sa=Sapphire, St=Steel. E.g.: AmSa = Amethyst/Sapphire, AbSt = Amber/Steel, EmSa = Emerald/Sapphire.
